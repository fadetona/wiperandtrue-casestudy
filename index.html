Kilograms of co2 recovered this year

We use cutting-edge technology to capture carbon dioxide produced during beer's fermentation, preventing its release into the atmosphere. The CO2 is cleaned, stored and reused elsewhere in our brewing and packaging processes, creating a closed loop and reducing our carbon footprint.
